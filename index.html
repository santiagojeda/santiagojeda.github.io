About Me

About me

My name is Santiago Ojeda Ramírez , I am a Biomedical Engineer, Digital Humanist and school Teacher. My research spans across different areas of knowledge like Video Games, Digital Humanities and Science Education.

In my masters thesis, under the advising of Prof. Carola Hernández, I designed and evaluated a Computational thinking course for Arts and Humanities' students.

During 2020 I have been a research assistant under the tutelage of Prof. Stephanie Toro, working on two exploratory studies within Uniandes' Science Faculty

I am interested on studying the design of learning enviroments for Computer Science Education under STEAM approaches, aiming to include different areas of knowledge to broad participation in CS towards students who may not have otherwise been exposed. Currently I am looking for a Ph.D. that can help me accomplish this goal.

When I am not working on my research I am learning to reading, playing video-games, and writing fiction.
